About Me

The ability to independently seek continuing education is paramount for any effective programmer.

Before I decided to make a career change, I had a 25 year work history in carpentry, construction, and environmental restoration fields, all of which require deliberate attention to detail, the ability to flexibly "zoom in and out" of the big picture down to the itemized particulars of a project, and the ability to both work in isolation and with a team to creatively solve problems. These skills, coupled with my notable work ethic, and commitment to furthering my education are directly transferrable to the tech field and will, no doubt, punctuate my ability to effectively fulfil any role for which I am hired.
